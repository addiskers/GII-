Automotive Wiring Harness Market Insights

Global Automotive Wiring Harness Market size was valued at USD 47.3 billion in 2022 and is poised to grow from USD 48.72 billion in 2023 to USD 61.33 billion by 2031, growing at a CAGR of 2.92% during the forecast period (2024-2031).

The global automotive wiring harness market is propelled by the increasing demand for advanced electrical and electronic systems in modern vehicles. The market aims to provide innovative and efficient wiring harness solutions to automotive manufacturers to ensure smooth and safe operations of various automotive systems. The primary driving factors for the automotive wiring harness market are the increasing demand for advanced safety and comfort features in vehicles, the growing trend of electric and hybrid vehicles, and the rising demand for fuel-efficient vehicles. The increasing adoption of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), infotainment systems, and other electronic systems in modern vehicles is also contributing to the growth of the market. The automotive wiring harness market faces several challenges such as the increasing complexity of automotive wiring systems, the risk of fire hazards due to faulty wiring, and the lack of standardization in the industry. The high cost of developing and testing wiring harnesses can also be a significant challenge for small and medium-sized market players. The automotive wiring harness market presents several growth opportunities, such as the increasing adoption of electric and hybrid vehicles, the growing demand for advanced safety and comfort features in vehicles, and the increasing trend of connected vehicles.

Automotive Wiring Harness Market Analysis by Vehicle

In 2022, light vehicles held the largest share in the automotive wiring harness market, accounting for over 90% of the total revenue. With the integration of advanced technologies and features in light vehicles such as GPS, infotainment systems, temperature control sensors, and tire pressure sensors, the demand for wiring harnesses in these vehicles is expected to increase while maintaining the optimum weight of the vehicle. Consumers now consider advanced functions and driving experience while purchasing a vehicle, which is redefining comfort and functionality while traveling. As a result, the demand for wiring harnesses in light vehicles is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.4% from 2023 to 2030.

The heavy vehicle segment is also expected to contribute to the growth of the market due to the increasing adoption of heavy-duty electric vehicles and the integration of telematics, GPS, and ADAS sensors and radar by truck and bus fleet managers. This is expected to augment the growth of the automotive wiring harness market in the heavy vehicle segment.

Automotive Wiring Harness Market Analysis by Application

In 2022, the chassis segment held the largest revenue share of over 34% in the automotive wiring harness market. The chassis harness system comprises the main, rear, and front harnesses, which contain wired connections to various multimedia systems, such as GPS, DVD, radio, engine, AC, and other sensors for temperature control and ADAS. The chassis harness system is responsible for providing wiring and connections to all the essential components that enable the vehicles to perform their primary functions. Moreover, the chassis harness system has the advantage of minimal risk of shorting, can withstand high temperatures, and improve fuel efficiency and vehicle performance.

Within the forecast period, the sensors sub-segment is expected to exhibit the highest growth rate of 6.0%. It includes various sensors for systems such as illumination, battery system, speed systems, infotainment systems, and ADAS. The wiring harness is equipped with sensors required to maintain and enable an analysis of relevant data. With the increasing use of sensors based on the level of autonomous driving technology in vehicles, the sensors sub-segment is expected to drive the growth of the market.

Automotive Wiring Harness Market Regional Insights

In 2022, the Asia Pacific region dominated the global automotive wiring harness market, accounting for the largest revenue share of 48.6%. This can be attributed to the presence of major automobile markets such as China and India, which are among the top 5 car-producing countries in the world. The high application of automotive wiring harnesses in the region is expected to further boost the market. Additionally, the region has a high population density and disposable income, and many manufacturers have a strong presence in the Asian market, launching advanced technological vehicles in the region.

Europe is expected to witness a healthy growth rate over the forecast period. The region is expected to experience a rise in the demand for high-voltage harness wires due to the consistent rise in sales of electric vehicles. The sales of EVs are further propelled by the subsidies provided by the EU and respective governments of European countries. This rise in EV sales is anticipated to positively impact the sales of wiring harnesses.

Increasing Trend of Integrated Advanced Features in Vehicles that Propels Market Growth

  • Todays cars are advanced with a large number of electronic integrations. By 2022, about 125 million vehicles are expected to be equipped with 5G connections that will hit the roads. The head-up-display units, infotainment systems, and dashboards in vehicles require many electronic and electric components. As this electronic equipment is becoming a standard accessory, car manufacturers and consumers have pushed the electronic component suppliers for more integrations and innovations. Todays consumers demand more intuitive technology, and they want an in-vehicle experience that allows them to stay connected, entertained, and comfortable.

Automotive Wiring Harness Market Restraints

Continuous Growing Risk of Wiring Harness Failures

  • The vehicle has various electrical failures, such as battery, lights, alternator, non-functional front & rear lights, and ignition failure, which can sometimes occur due to wiring harness failures. Sometimes, the wiring harness bends due to the rapidly changing environments like extreme cold to severely hot temperatures, as well as rapid shifts from one temperature level to another. These problems can corrode the overall wiring harness & largely decrease the rate of its overall lifespan.

Corrosive Nature of Wiring Harness Hurdle to Hinder Growth

  • The issues of reliability and durability associated with the incorporation of wiring harnesses in EVs are projected to hinder the market growth. Further, the corrosive nature of the automotive wiring harness makes it challenging to incorporate into EVs. These factors, in turn, are estimated to restrain in the coming years. Additionally, the manufacturers must invest comprehensively in the research and development of advanced wiring harnesses, which may likely limit the market growth over the forecast period.

Automotive Wiring Harness Key Market Trends

  • Increasing use of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS): the automotive wiring harness market is the increasing use of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) in vehicles. ADAS includes features such as lane departure warning, automatic emergency braking, and adaptive cruise control, which require a complex network of sensors and wiring to function properly. The demand for ADAS is expected to rise due to the increasing focus on vehicle safety and the growing number of regulations mandating the installation of safety features in vehicles.
  • Growing demand for electric vehicles: The rising trend of electric vehicles (EVs) is impacting the automotive wiring harness market. As electric vehicles have a different powertrain than traditional vehicles, their wiring harnesses require different specifications, materials, and designs to handle the high voltage and power. The demand for EV wiring harnesses is expected to rise due to the increasing number of EV models being launched in the market. The increasing government initiatives to reduce carbon emissions and promote electric vehicle adoption are expected to boost the demand for automotive wiring harnesses in the EV market.
